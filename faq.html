Need help? Browse our FAQs

Any question's you may have are potentially already answered here, alternatively please feel free to email us at gammerappdevelopments@gmail.com and we will respond ASAP and post any FAQ's in our help center.

Do you provide any other services than web or mobile development?

Yes we also provide marketing services and business consultations to identify how to achieve business goals such as increase sales, improve efficiency and grow the business. If you wish to find out more please drop an email to gammerappdevelopment@gmail.com

Why should we choose to buy with you?

Great question! The application development market for both web and mobile is very saturated and there is plenty of competition. The reason you should choose us is because we are a relatively new business therefore we are focused on ensuring we do whatever it takes to ensure our customers are as happy as can be! Despite being new as a business, our team consists of experts with years of experience so there is no need to worry! Customer satisfaction is at the heart of what we do as we wish to build up a series of good testemonials.

Are your team qualified for the job?

Yes. Our team consists of university graduates and freelance professionals with years of experience to match the perfect blend of youthful ideas with industry experience.

Can you provide us with back end functionality, for example a database to store information for our customers?

Of course! Our lead database developer has over 4 years experience in designing and implementing secure databases.

Why do you not display pricing on your site?

We do not have set prices because we determine them based on factors such as the market, our competitors, estimated time taken and complexity of the task. We always offer a free quote and agree to match or beat our competitors prices whilst always maintaining the upmost professionalism and providing a service which makes you feel comfortable. This includes regular project phone/skpe calls, in person meetings and email discussions.

Can you provide us with your own design?

Absolutely! The initial stages will consist of our project manager sitting down with you to discuss the requirements of the site, including how you want it to look and feel. If you already have a design in mind that's great we will work with you to draw it up and create a prototype so you can visualise it better. Alternatively, we can create a design from scratch based on what you describe to us.

How do we know the transaction will be secure?

We do not ask you to provide the full payment up front. We only ask that you provide an initial payment to secure our services, this will be a percentage of the total cost. The final payment will be made upon completion of the project when you are fully satisfied with the product and then the handover will be complete.

How will I be able to monitor the progress of the project?

As promised you will receive your own project manager who will be available to you almost 24 hours a day. The project manager will have access to all your agile development tools including project plans, backlogs, effort estimation graphs etc. These documents can be provided on request and will give you a rough idea of the guidelines we will aim to stick to.

How will we host the website?

We assist you with hosting the website as well! You can have your own domain name, choosing from yourname.com, .net, .co.uk etc and loads more! We will work with you to ensure you get the best price for however long you wish to host the website for.
